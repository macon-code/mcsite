Help us design for Macon

We are Macon Code, a non-profit organization working on solutions for community problems through the thoughtful use of technology. We beleive in the possibilities of our community, and we strive to develop resources that make Macon better for everyone.

Who can join?

You can!

Our group is entirely volunteer based, and our members come from various backgrounds and experiences. We are always looking for fresh ideas and new perspectives. We believe all our community members have something to offer as we design, develop, code, and implement projects.

Enjoy technology?

So do we! Join us to put your skills to work for the Macon community!
